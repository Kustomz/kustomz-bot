# Customer Portal

The official Kustomz Mod Shop Discord server serves as the Customer Portal for all mod services.

Customers can:

- Open private service tickets
- Communicate directly with staff
- Receive updates on active requests
- Stay informed through announcements and public community channels

---

## Private Ticket System

Every service request begins with a private ticket.

Only the customer and authorized staff members have access to an active service ticket, ensuring organized and confidential communication throughout the process.
