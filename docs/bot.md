# Kustomz Bot

The Kustomz Bot is an integral part of the Kustomz Mod Shop customer experience.

Its primary purpose is to support the Discord customer portal through automation, organization, and server management.

---

## Responsibilities

The Kustomz Bot supports:

- Ticket automation
- Server management
- Community management
- Operational efficiency

---

## Purpose

The Kustomz Bot helps provide a professional, organized, and consistent experience for both customers and staff throughout the service process.
